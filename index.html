Tow-Go

A Unique Waste Management Concept

Collects Garbage

Tow-go is an all-in-one, independent, multi-functional and intelligent waste management machine. It is a moving Wet Waste Composter, which itself goes to the garbage and collects it.

Digestion Process

It then shreds and dewaters the wet waste, and further converts it into dry, environment-friendly soil. Once the waste is collected, Tow-go can be parked at any convenient place for the digestion process.

Dry Soil Formation

After the initial 8 days, good quality dry soil can be removed every day from Tow-go and then it is ready to move around to collect waste for another day.

Benefits

⦿ No waste needs to go out of the territory of generation, it is one of the best decentralised waste management systems

⦿ Waste does not need to be handled by workers, thus saving them from a health hazard.

⦿ Since Tow-go treats only wet waste, the user will by default arrange for segregation. Segregated dry waste can easily be managed and disposed as most of it is usually recyclable.

⦿ Use of black plastic garbage bags will be reduced and collection of garbage in bins will be promoted.

⦿ No harm to environment, 100% green initiative

Unique Features

⦿ It picks up and disposes the waste in de-centralised way

⦿ ZERO fixed space is required for processing, no dumping ground is required

⦿ Waste reduction to compost is between 80% to 85% (1000 kg waste will give 200 kg-150 kg of compost)

⦿ No shabbiness, no odour and the waste is not even visible while it is being processed
⦿ Entire process is run on the engine of the vehicle

⦿ Vehicle is run on green fuel called CNG, even the part of heat generated by the engine is absorbed and used for the processing of compost

⦿ Smart Tow-go will be operated through PLC and any disfunction will immediately be messaged/ intimated to the service provider who can remotely solve most of the issues within a few seconds, without even visiting the site

⦿ ZERO manpower is required for processing of waste- 1 driver and 2 labourers are required only for collection. Once Tow-go is parked, it will operate automatically and no manual intervention is required

⦿ It has its own security system, equipped with serveillance cameras, theft control alarm system and immediate reporting device

⦿ 24 hrs tracking and monitoring system at centralised office of service provider

⦿ Most of the electrical functions are taken care of by the electricity generated by solar panels, fixed on top of the vehicle

⦿ Tow-go is going to absorb day-time heat through SOLAR AIR HEATING TECHNOLOGY, patented by IIT-Powai.
